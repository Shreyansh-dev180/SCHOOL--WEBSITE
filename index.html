ABOUT US

DRASI Paramount School is dedicated to providing the blend of both thought and action. DRASI Paramount School, Devlaspur, Kerakat, Jaunpur endeavors to build up an ambiance of reverence for education and a healthy environment, which have brought the school among the best schools in Devlaspur, Kerakat, Jaunpur.

As per our motto **“Service Before Self”**, we pledge to leave no stone unturned to provide opportunities for our students to be prepared and trained to lead the society in all sectors. Right from its initial days, the vision was to develop a center of excellence that caters the best school education in Devlaspur, Kerakat, Jaunpur to the local crowd of Uttar Pradesh.

Within a short span of time, DRASI Paramount School has been recognized as one of the best schools in Devlaspur as it molds work, sports, and co-curricular activities so that the students spur to be the brightest and the best.
